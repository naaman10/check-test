About checkmirrors

Checkmirrors was established in 2014. The company director is Sarah Hellowell who has over 14 years experience instructing.

We are a small company with experienced driving instructors in York who give total quality tuition assuring complete safety for each pupil.

Our aim is not just to teach people to pass a driving test but to teach a skill for life so when they leave us they are a safe and competent driver.
